Vansh Enterprises

Vinsul is a leading trader of insulation, fasteners, coatings & fire protection products

Vinsul is a partnership firm dealing with leading manufacturers of insulation, fasteners, coatings, and fire protection products in India with a track record of providing high-quality insulation products for domestic and export markets over the past five years.

We deal in many products made from volcanic rock, one of nature's most abundant resources. We rely on it not only because it is a naturally renewable and sustainable material, but also because it combines a unique combination of benefits.

Our insulation products deliver outstanding fire, acoustic, thermal and acoustic insulation properties with a lifelong durability, making them a cost-effective and environmentally friendly choice. Insulation saves the environment, buildings, and more than the energy used in its production. The use of this technology is beneficial to the environment as it conserves scarce energy resources.

Supreme Industries Limited is India's leading plastic processing company with seven business divisions. The company has forayed into different types of plastic processing in Injection Moulding, Rotational Moulding (ROTO), Extrusion, Compression Moulding, Blow Moulding etc.

Feukem India Pvt. Ltd is a leading manufacturer of Water Borne Fire Retardant Coating for GI/MS Duct Sheet, Steel Structures and Electrical Cables, Fire Retardant Liquids, Anticorrosive coatings and Firestop solutions. We pride ourselves on durable solutions for projects and customers, which are specially formulated to meet the industry demands.

ROXUL ROCKWOOL India makes and supplies a full range of smart and sustainable insulation products for the construction and process industries based on innovative stone wool technology.

The EJOT Group is on a global growth path, combined with intensive construction activity characterised by the creation of modern industrial architecture. Towards the end of the 1970s, the product range of EJOT included elements for fixing insulating elements for thermal insulation in house construction. In this sector, EJOT developed into the market leader and covers about 80 percent of the demand in Germany.

Twiga is the leading manufacturer of glass wool insulation in India having a track record of more than forty years of supplying to domestic and export customers with world-class products for various applications of thermal and acoustic insulation. It is a technology licensee of ISOVER Saint-Gobain, the leading manufacturer of insulations worldwide. Twiga is the only Insulation product manufacturer in India offering ISI marked products.
